SitStayGo App Design

Leaving a pet behind isn’t easy for pet owners. SitStayGo is an app that makes it easier and less stressful for pet owners to find trustworthy pet sitters in their area. SitStayGo includes both boarding facilities as well as individual pet sitters in the area and allows the user to complete the entire process in one app.

Minnesota Now Website Redesign

Minnesota NOW’s website was designed to help women and allies take action through intersectional grassroots activism to promote feminist ideals, lead societal change, eliminate discrimination, and protect the equal rights of all. We redesigned the Minnesota NOW website to create a more seamless and intuitive user experience to help users find the relevant information and resources they need and increase engagement for their nonprofit.

FCC Website Redesign

Penny Plates App Design

It is difficult for people to plan and cook nutritious meals on a regular basis because it can be time consuming, expensive, and confusing. Penny Plates automatically plans healthy affordable meals, educates users on nutrition, and creates a grocery list. It’s an app for personalized weekly meal plans that won’t hurt your wallet!
